Payment & Security

We created from ground up, an innovative Pre-pay guarantee system to ensure that project owners and professionals feel confident in the project's funding.

  1. Funds are made available after contract and milestones are agreed upon.
  2. Funds are held in a secure account while the professionals work on the project.
  3. Once the owner receives the deliverables, funds are released to the professionals.

    4. "There is an escrow service to protect you."

  4. Your project is spilt into milestones with expected deliverables meeting world-class standard.
  5. Payments only happens when deliverables meet these standards.
  6. Dispute resolutions are handled by Devcenter, down to the full return of milestone or project fees.
