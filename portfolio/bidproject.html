Helping contractors work better.

Our client wanted anyone with a construction project receive and review bids from potential contractors, manage project timelines and handle contractor payouts .

Problem in the construction industry?

Applications used in the construction industry are monolithic feature packed, enterprise grade technologies designed for the PC era. Most of which are extremely difficult to use, have a steep learning curve and are not compatible with the current technological & payment infrastructures available today.

How did we approach solving this challenge?

We applied a mix of User personas and job-to-be-done design methods. We ran through 2 design sprints that required us understanding the problem and users, diverging to find the best ideas, deciding on which ideas made the most sense, prototyping these ideas and validating them.

Implementing the ideas

Proper enquiry on the potential user base for this application, revealed an older demography between the ages of 35-60. This influenced our use of a larger and more legible type font, white space and colored icons.

What was the result from the challenge?

We were able to design an extremely simple application that met all the requirements with almost no learning curve, that was compatible with modern technologies.
