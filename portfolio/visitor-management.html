Automating visitor management.

Vesuvius, a new startup targeted at the real estate, reached out to Devcenter to help build a solution that automates and facilitates visitor management.

Problems with managing visitors?

Visitor management within communities in Nigeria are still mostly manual, disjointed and untrackable. It is used majorly as a smoke mirror to create a feeling of security. Acting on data, if any, is daunting.

Identifying and understanding…

This first step was to identify the target audience and determine the best way to provide the solution.

We took the design first approach.

We iterated design over several sprints while the client performed user testing to get feedback on our design decisions.

Implementing on the web

As an MVP, a Progressive Web App was a great approach - An app like feel, quicker implementation, shorter time to market, no need for app stores, updates can be experienced in real time.

What did we get as result?

We were able to build a truly simple application that enables members of a community manage their visitors creating a truly secure environment.
